About us

An independent oversight platform

The Social Security Complaint Site (SSCS) was established to create a transparent, accessible channel for citizens to report malpractice in health and administrative services.

We operate independently, using a rigorous review process to ensure every complaint is handled fairly and efficiently, regardless of the institution involved.

Our investigation team works alongside legal and healthcare professionals to ensure your complaints lead to real accountability.
